Tour schedule

FIRST DAY : PHU QUOC - GRAND WORLD Number of meals: 02 meals (Lunch, Dinner)

In the morning : You gather at Da Nang airport, Domestic station. Tour guide will check-in for you to take flight to Phu Quoc. Pick up at the airport, transfer to check in at VinOasis Phu Quoc Luxury Resort. In the afternoon : You can freely explore some items at the Grand World area (expenses excluded) such as: bamboo works, contemporary art park of Open Park...; Stroll along the "Venice canal" and see the gondolas, the magnificent castle gate, three semi-circular arch bridges... Group dinner buffet, rest in Phu Quoc.

SECOND DAY : FULL PLAY AT VINWONDERS PHU QUOC THEME PARK - SAFARI NATURE RESERVATION Number of meals: 03 meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

In the morning : You have breakfast buffet at VinOasis. After breakfast, you will come to explore Vinpearl Safari Phu Quoc - the first Semi-Wild Zoo in Vietnam - with a scale of 180ha, with more than 130 species of rare animals and programs of Animal Performances, Photography. Photo with animals, Explore and experience the Open Zoo in the natural forest, close and friendly to humans.

In the morning : You have breakfast buffet at VinOasis, free to swim and rest until hotel check out time. The car takes you back to the center of Duong Dong town for lunch.

FINALLY DAY: PHU QUOC - Da Nang Number of meals: 02 meals (Breakfast, Lunch)

In the afternoon : After that, you will visit Dinh Cau: a cultural and religious symbol of Phu Quoc Island. A place where local fishermen put their trust for a trip to the sea to catch fish full of fish when they return. Visiting and shopping for souvenirs at Duc Thanh Tieu Garden, Fish sauce barrel house, Phu Quoc pearl production facility. Admire and shop for genuine pearl jewelry cultured in Phu Quoc. Dong Tam Snake Farm 2: the center for research and conservation of precious snakes on the island. Here, you are introduced, learn about snakes: cobra, king cobra, krait, red-tailed viper... and experience taking pictures with tamed pythons. The car will take you to the airport for a flight back to Da Nang. Farewell to you and end the tour program at Tan Son Nhat airport.
